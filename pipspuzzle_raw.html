💡 How to Play Arrows Puzzle Escape

🎯 Objective

Remove all arrows from the grid by clicking on them in the correct order. Each arrow can only move in the direction it's pointing!

📝 Game Rules

  1. Click an Arrow: Click on any arrow to attempt to move it.
  2. Path Must Be Clear: The arrow can only escape if its path in the direction it's pointing is completely clear of other arrows.
  3. Strategy is Key: You must remove arrows in the correct sequence. Some arrows block others!
  4. Lives System: Clicking a blocked arrow costs you a life. Run out of lives and it's game over!
  5. Beat the Timer: Complete the puzzle before time runs out (can be disabled).

🎮 Controls & Settings

  • Difficulty: Choose Easy (10x10), Medium (16x16), or Hard (20x20) grid sizes
  • Shape Modes: Play on different shaped grids - Heart, Star, Circle, Diamond, or standard rectangle
  • Theme Toggle: Switch between Colorful and Classic themes
  • Timer: Enable or disable the countdown timer
  • 💡 Hint: Click the Hint button or press 'H' to highlight a movable arrow (lasts 5 seconds)

💡 Pro Tips

  • Look for arrows at the edges first - they're often easiest to remove
  • Trace each arrow's path to see what it blocks
  • Work backwards: find arrows that aren't blocking anything
  • Stuck? Use the Hint button (or press 'H') to get help finding your next move!
  • Take your time! Every puzzle is guaranteed to be solvable

🏆 Win Condition

Remove all arrows from the grid before time runs out and without losing all your lives. Good luck!
